2. BASIC AI SIMULATOR (Fast)
   - Poisson-based statistical model
   - ~10ms per match
   - 20 Premier League team profiles
   - Perfect for: Large ML training datasets (1000+ matches)
   
3. ADVANCED TACTICAL SIMULATOR (Realistic)
   - Minute-by-minute event simulation
   - 6 formations: 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 3-5-2, 4-2-3-1, 3-4-3, 5-3-2
   - 6 playing styles: Possession, Counter-attack, High Press, etc.
   - Match dynamics: Momentum, Energy, Morale
   - Event tracking: Goals, shots, saves, fouls, cards, corners
   - 10+ team attributes (attack, defense, pressing, pace, etc.)
   - Two modes: Detailed (~150ms) or Quick (~20ms)
   - Perfect for: Realistic match analysis, tactical studies
